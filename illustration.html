Illustration Portfolio

This is a collection of my scientific and editorial illustrations from my time in the University of Washington Scientific Illustration Certificate Program and as Art Director for the undergraduate neuroscience journal Grey Matters that I helped found. I have found that illustration and design pose similar visual communication challenges. Both require research about the user and the source material to identify important features. Then the illustrator or designer must interpret this information, choosing what to emphasize and what to omit, for simple and clear communication. I use this process to illustrate both natural forms and abstract concepts like prosopagnosia and personality.
