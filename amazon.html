Amazon Internship

I interned at Amazon.com during Summer 2015, where I designed a prototype for a feature that isn't released yet.

Problem

Design a feature for Amazon Marketing Services.

Action

  • Conducted and compiled competitive research.
  • Sat in on user studies and contributed to interpretation of results.
  • Developed a prototype in Axure using competitive research, user study findings, and feedback solicited from fellow designers, developers, and leadership.
  • Delivered an Axure prototype for user testing and identified and implemented improvements for the final version.
  • Designed graphics and layout for production ready prototype.

Result

The feature hasn't been released yet, but users expressed enthusiasm for it during testing. I also successfully advocated for the most user-centered of the solutions proposed to leadership.
